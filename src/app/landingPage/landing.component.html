WHY BE A CULTURIST?

Share Your Knowledge and Culture

Do you have unique local knowledge and culture you would like to share? There are many travellers that would love to experience and learn about your way of life.

Meet Travellers

Being a culturist will give you the opportunity to meet, connect with and make friends with people from around the world. You may find opportunities for collaborate with them.

Earn Money

You can charge travellers to share experiences with you. We hope that this gives you more options to be able to sustain your cultural heritage, environment and way of life.
